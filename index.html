How The Commission System Works

Simple 5-step process from purchase to delivery

1

Buy a Token

Visit the SkyBlock shop /pw kma and purchase a Map Art Commission Token. The token will appear in your inventory once purchased.

Location: /pw kma → Commissions
2

Contact Me

Reach out NilaDoze through Discord or in-game messaging. Have your token ready and the image you want converted.

  • In-game: /msg NilaDoze [message]
  • Discord: Available 24/7
3

Redeem Token

Trade me the token to activate your commission slot. I'll confirm the details and add you to the queue.

Required: Token + Image file
4

Creation

I build your map art using professional tools and high-quality block palettes optimized for Minecraft maps.

Tools: Litematica, Rebane
5

Delivery

Receive your completed map art in-game. I'll meet you at your island or a designated location to hand over the maps.

Includes: All materials & setup

Available Map Sizes

Choose the perfect size for your image

Single Map

1×1 (128×128 blocks)

Perfect for profile pictures, icons, and small logos. Best for simple images with minimal detail.

Medium Canvas

2×2 (256×256 blocks)

Ideal for medium-sized artwork with more detail. Great for character portraits and scenic views.

Large Canvas

3×3 (384x384 blocks)

Maximum detail for complex images. Perfect for large artwork, posters, and high-detail photography.

Portrait

2×3 (256x384 blocks)

A taller layout for images that are naturally vertical. Perfect for portrait images, banners, vertical illustrations, or in-game posters.

Landscape

3×2 (384x256 blocks)

A wider layout ideal for landscapes, panoramic scenes, logos, or group portraits. Allows wide designs to fit without being compressed, keeping all key elements visible.

Custom Sizes Available

Need a specific size like 1×2 or 4×4? Contact me for custom dimensions tailored to your project.

Good to Know

Important details about the commission process

Materials Included

All materials are provided at no extra cost, which is reflected in the overall project pricing.

Payment

Flat rates, per-buy rates, or rank trades are allowed as commission payments.

One Token Rule

You will have to wait for your current map to be completed before requesting another.

Image Format

Any image format accepted (JPG, PNG, etc.). Higher resolution source images produce better results.

Custom Sizes

Non-standard sizes available upon request. Pricing may vary based on complexity and dimensions.

Quality Guarantee

Preview provided before final delivery. Minor adjustments included if needed.
